Email format — how leads send scheduling requests

All flows are triggered by email to connect@uvidconsulting.com. The subject line determines which flow fires. The body provides the meeting details. Every field must be on its own line exactly as shown.

  • ONE-OFF MEETING: Subject: Schedule Meeting
  • RECURRING MEETING: Subject: Schedule Recurring Meeting
  • RESCHEDULE ONE OCCURRENCE: Subject: Reschedule Meeting
  • RESCHEDULE FULL SERIES: Subject: Reschedule Series
  • CANCEL ONE OCCURRENCE: Subject: Cancel Meeting
  • CANCEL FULL SERIES: Subject: Cancel Series
  • ADD PARTICIPANT: Subject: Add Participant
  • CHANGE MEETING TITLE: Subject: Update Meeting
This automatically sets the email subject to trigger Power Automate.
